Meet

Pastor Femi Olawale

DCO & Country Coordinator, RCCG Canada
Pastor Femi Olawale is a man of unwavering faith and commitment to the gospel of Jesus Christ. He believes that with God, all things are possible. Hence his catchphrase "impossibility does not exist with God." This unwavering belief in the power of God has allowed him to overcome countless challenges and obstacles throughout his life and ministry, and it has inspired countless others to do the same.

(613) 733-1837

info@femiolawale.com

As a pastor who has dedicated his life to serving the Lord and spreading His message of love and redemption to the world, Pastor Femi Olawale is a man of great authority. He is not a man of earthly power, but rather a man who has completely submitted his life to the rulership of Jesus Christ. This submission is evident in all that he does, as he strives to live a life that is fully surrendered to the Lord
Pastor Femi Olawale's loyalty and commitment to the Lord are unwavering, and he is deeply committed to nurturing and cultivating the faith of others. He is a faithful shepherd to his flock, tending to the spiritual needs of his congregation with love and compassion. And just as he cares for the seeds that God has placed in his hands, he also works tirelessly to plant the seeds of faith in the hearts of those around him, helping them to grow and flourish in their own relationship with the Lord.
Through his tireless work in the ministry, he has become a beacon of hope and inspiration for many, and his impact on the lives of those he serves will be felt for generations to come. He is a true man of God, dedicated to serving the Lord with all his heart, soul, and strength.
